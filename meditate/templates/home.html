If you're truly interested in developing a healing and transformative meditation practice, this book is a great option.  Written as a series of 12 chapters with each chapter guiding and deepening your practice using a different form of meditation for that week.  You'll need to commit 10-20 minutes twice a day.  Each chapter provides clear instructions focusing on a different element of meditation or meditative practices.  At the end of the course you will have the basis of a steady practice and a good idea what particular meditative practices are best suited for you.

Why Meditate?

While the book references meditation practices from various traditions it is non-denominational, requiring no adoption of religious beliefs.  I encourage you to try it and see first-hand if it's valuable.  Returns are accepted within 30 days on all purchases.

Mentoring is available for those that would like support to develop a solid practice.  For those planning on 20 minutes or more per day and interested in a significant amount of support, "Deep Diving" mentoring is arranged for two 30-minute video chat sessions per week, one toward the beginning of each week to make sure you get off on the right foot and the second toward the middle of each week to make sure you're understanding and getting the most out of your meditation sessions with personalized suggestions based on your experience.

For those that just need a little help and someone encouraging them to stick with the practice, there's "Check-In" mentoring for one 15-minute videochat session per week.
